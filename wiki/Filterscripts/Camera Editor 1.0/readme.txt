Camera Editor 1.0
by 009 (Alex009)

Description:
Edit your camera in game.Controll keys:
> Look behind - on/off editor
> Up - move front
> Down - move back
> Left - rotate left
> Right - rotate right
> Sprint - rotate up
> Crouch - rotate down
> Analog left - speed up
> Analog right - speed down
> Walk - save current pos
